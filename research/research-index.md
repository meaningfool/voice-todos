# Research Index

Topics to explore in depth, organized by roadmap item. Each topic captures what we know so far, what's unclear, and links to a dedicated file when the research is done.

## How We Do Research

Research is conversational. For each topic, we go through the questions one by one, discussing each in a back-and-forth before moving on. The written report is only created at the end, once the conversation is complete — filtering, restructuring, and dropping questions that turned out to be less relevant along the way.
